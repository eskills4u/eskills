About ‘eSkills4U’


‘eSkills4U’ (which stems from “e-skills for you”) is the only personalized skilling training platform for homemakers with a mission to empower her digital skills, kick start online business and achieve financial independence. We work exclusively with homemakers from rural and urban rural areas to turn their hobby into an online profitable business and achieve financial independence. In turn, to provide a complete eco-system for every homemaker, small and medium business owners.
Unpaid work imposes cost in the form of missed opportunities for education, skill acquisition or improvement, and public participation. Homemakers are a largely forgotten audience. Homemakers have a desire to be respected, to be financially independent and above all, to be recognized. There are over 200 million literate homemakers in urban India out of which 148 million are not employed or studying. ‘eSkills4U’ plans to make homemakers financially independent in the coming years.
That’s why our vision is to empower Indian Women through education to earn recognition, respect and financial independence. Upon an in-depth analysis of our market, we’ve come to know that there are over 200 million literate homemakers in urban India out of which 148 million are not employed or studying. The Pandemic has shown significant growth in online business. Markets have grown, and so have the ideas of making money. It is a great time for homemakers to start a business, earn money online, share knowledge, art, and start a new income stream following a passion and be recognized.
To fuel our growth and help us hit our goals, we’re seeking a 50 Lakhs investment in our company. This will help us cover our startup costs and provide us with approximately one year’s worth of operating capital while we work to recoup our initial investment, with an ultimate goal of breaking even in two years.
